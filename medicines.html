Common Medicine Information

Popular Medicines & Health Products

Paracetamol

Commonly used for fever and mild pain relief.

Cetirizine

Commonly used for allergy symptoms like sneezing, itching, and runny nose.

ORS

Used to help manage dehydration due to vomiting, diarrhea, or heat exhaustion.

Antacid

Used for acidity, heartburn, and indigestion relief.

Cough Syrup

Used for cough relief depending on the type of cough. Ask before use.

Vitamin D3

Supports bone health and vitamin D levels.

Calcium Supplements

Used to support bone and teeth health.

First Aid Items

Bandages, antiseptic solutions, cotton, gauze, and basic wound care products.
